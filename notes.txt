## Circuit

7 - SS RFID
8 - RST RFID

20 (A2) - SCE Screen
19 (A1) - RST Screen
18 (A0) - D/C Screen
15 - SCLK
14 - MISO
16 - MOSI
10 - LED Screen Backlight (PWM) (w/ 330 Ohm resistor)

## Dependencies

Install the "Adafruit GFX Library" and the "Adafruit PCD8544 Nokia 5110 LCD library"
